ScoreKeeper

Capstone Project

Rachel Cavin

The Problem

Most teachers use paper or spreadsheets for grading, storing, and creating assignments.

Ineffeciencies in the scoring and assesment process robs time they could allocate to instructional design.

Research indicates of those who go into teaching, 40-50% are gone within 5 years.

ScoreKeeper

Early stage product that aims to reduce the time teachers spend on assessment and scoring.

Gradebook functionality for managing grades, students, courses, and assignments.

Future development plans that include automatically graded multiple choice assessments, simplified rubric creation and grading, and better student progress monitoring systems.

Features

Gradebook that tracks student grades on assignments

Roster for managing enrollment across all classes

Assignments table that makes assigning work as easy as entering the due date

Class manager for simple class list updates

Student dashboard that makes grades communication instantaneous

Teacher managed log in credentials for students
